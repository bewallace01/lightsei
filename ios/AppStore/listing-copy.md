## Description (max 4000)

```
Lightsei is your team of AI coworkers, in your pocket.

Instead of one general-purpose chatbot, Lightsei lets you assemble a team of specialized AI coworkers, each focused on the work it is best at, and talk to them from a simple chat interface. Ask a question, hand off a task, and watch the right coworker pick it up.

Built for teams that care where their data goes. Lightsei ships with trust zones and per-team isolation as defaults, not upgrades, so the coworker that talks to the outside world is kept separate from the one that touches sensitive records.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

- Chat with your AI coworkers in a clean, familiar interface.
- Switch between the people and teams you work with from one place.
- Operators can monitor activity and review what their coworkers have been doing from the Activity view.
- Get a reply where you are, on your phone, without opening a laptop.

WHY LIGHTSEI

- Specialized over general. A focused coworker for each kind of work beats one bot that does everything passably.
- Trust zones by default. Sensitive data and the open internet do not have to share a context.
- Easy by default, powerful when you grow. Most people never leave the chat. Advanced teams can configure deeper from the web dashboard.

GETTING STARTED

Sign in with the account you created at lightsei.com, or accept an invite from a team that already uses Lightsei, and start chatting.

Lightsei works alongside the Lightsei web dashboard, where teams configure and deploy their coworkers. This app is the chat surface for talking to them day to day.

Questions or feedback? Visit https://app.lightsei.com/support
```
